    F. Long-term Financial Levers

    • Manure / organic amendments: may reduce synthetic fertilizer cost over time.
    • Cover crops: can improve soil health and reduce input needs in future seasons.
    • Non-GMO seed: in some regions lowers seed cost/acre while maintaining acceptable yield.
    • Crop diversification: spreading risk across multiple crop types reduces price exposure.
